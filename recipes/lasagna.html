Lasagna recipe

Description

This recipe will help you prepare easy cheesy skillet lasagna.

It can serve a total of six people.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 pot hot Italian sauce
  • 1 medium red bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cups sliced mushrooms
  • 1 (24 ounce) jar, Ragu速 Old World Style速 traditional sauce
  • 1 (9 ounce) package, no-boil lasagna sheets, broken into large pieces
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed diced roasemary
  • 8 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 8 ounces part-skim mozzarella, shredded and divided
  • 2 tablespoons, minced fresh Italian parsley

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium high and add olive oil. Swirl to coat.

    Add the onion and saute until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the sausage, red pepper, and mushrooms; saute until meat is cooked through, about 5 minutes.

  2. Stir in the Ragu速 Old World Style速 Traditional sauce, broken lasagna noodles, water, and rosemary.

    Cover and turn heat down to medium-low.

  3. Cook for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the pasta is al dente. Remove the lid and add the ricotta, 2/3 of the cheese, and parsley; stir to combine.

    Top with remaining mozzarella and serve immediately.
